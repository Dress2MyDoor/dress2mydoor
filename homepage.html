About Us

Angie, Sophie and Belle welcome you to our pre-loved, occasion wear for hire or re-sale business, based at Glebe Farm in Congleton. We promote sustainability, inclusivity and support cost of living, making your 'Cinderella Moment' affordable whatever your size, style or budget requirements. We have recently acquired a large crop of ex sample Freda Bennett and Justin Alexander dresses, many of which are available for re-sale. We accept donations and our promise to you is that if we re-hire your dress, the fact it was donated will be reflected in the price. If at some point we sell the dress on then this money will be put towards a charitable donation or project .
